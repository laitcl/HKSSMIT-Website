Event List

The Hong Kong Student Society of MIT hosts often events associated to Hong Kong culture, or occassionally out of sheer tradition. The following are a list of events that take place on a regular basis.

  • Welcome Dinner - September
  • Mid-Autumn Festival - September
  • Foliage - October
  • Halloween - October
  • Thanksgiving - November
  • Winter Dinner - December
  • Christmas Party - December
  • New Year Countdown - January
  • Chinese New Year Celebration - February
  • Spring Break Retreat - March
  • Easter Celebration - April
  • Pre-Final Study Break - May
  • Commencement Farewell - June
  • Summer Meetup - July/August
