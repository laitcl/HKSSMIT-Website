The 2015-2016 Board's First Event

Lawrence Lai
Saturday, 24th October 2015

We experience quite a difference here in the shoes of the officers. Once upon a time, we were the participants that enjoy the food and sense of community that takes place in HKSS events; now, there is the extra pleasure of seeing ourselves shape the community, at the cost of the hassle behind the event.

Our new team, Alice and I, seem to have a slightly different vision of HKSS than previous officers. Our objectives are slightly less oriented towards the benefits that we are providing HKSS members, but more towards the intrinsic experience of our community, to say the least.

And hence, this event looked no different from any other, but it meant a lot for our new board. We definitely look forward to our next event, coming up on the weekend of Halloween where we play horror video games.
